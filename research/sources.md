# Research Sources — AI-Powered SEO Content Production for B2B SaaS

## Topic
How B2B SaaS companies are using AI tools to produce, optimise, 
and scale SEO content — while maintaining quality, topical authority, 
and visibility across both traditional search engines and generative 
AI platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity.

## Why This Topic
Search is changing faster than most marketers realise. 
Traditional keyword-led content is being replaced by 
AI-assisted content systems that build topical authority at scale. 
For B2B SaaS companies this represents both a threat and an 
opportunity — the brands that build the right content infrastructure 
now will dominate organic acquisition for the next decade.

## Why These Experts
Each expert was selected based on three criteria:
1. They actively practise what they teach — not just write about it
2. They publish regularly with verifiable, data-backed insights
3. They specifically address the intersection of AI and SEO 
   for B2B or SaaS contexts
