## Post 1
**Date:** 2 days ago
**Topic:** Agents are a multiplier on judgment — 
not a replacement for it

**Content Summary:**
"Everyone's selling agents as 'do the same work 
with fewer people.' It's exactly backwards."

Key argument:
- Agents multiply judgment — they do not replace it
- Most organisations do not have enough judgment 
  to survive being multiplied
- "Hand 20 agents to a mediocre team and you 
  don't get leverage. You get 20x the confident 
  slop, shipped faster than anyone can catch it."

**Why this matters for AI-powered SEO content production:**
The most direct warning in this entire research project 
against naive AI content production at scale. 
20x content output from a mediocre content strategy 
is 20x mediocre content — not 20x results. 
AI content production requires strong editorial 
judgment as the foundation — exactly what 
Kieran Flanagan called the intelligence layer 
and Eli Schwartz called uniqueness.
