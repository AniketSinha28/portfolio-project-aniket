## Post 1
**Date:** 2 hours ago (19/06/2026)
**Topic:** Own the intelligence — rent the model

**Content Summary:**
Flanagan argues that the biggest mistake marketers make is 
outsourcing their marketing intelligence to AI models. The same 
advice Claude gives you is being given to every competitor in 
your vertical. Real competitive advantage comes from your 
proprietary Marketing Intelligence Loop — three nodes:
1. Marketing Judgment — cannot be prompted, built through experience
2. Intelligence Layer — your biggest asset, lives in your best 
   employees, leaves with them
3. AI Models — pluggable execution only, rent them, do not own them

Key quote: "Own the intelligence; rent the model"

**Why this matters for AI-powered SEO content production:**
This is the most important framing for AI content strategy. 
Teams that use AI to produce generic SEO content will get 
generic results. The teams that win will feed their proprietary 
customer intelligence, ICP data, and content experiments INTO 
the AI — producing content competitors cannot replicate.
